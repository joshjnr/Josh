About us


Adopt a child foundation is home to up to 200 orphaned, destitute and abandoned babies and children at any one time
These projects continue to enrich the lives of many hundreds of Kenyan children and adults every day.
Through Adopt a child foundation, we help around 1700 children in Kenya every year. We have a committed team of 200 Kenyan staff including
teachers, house mothers, management and social workers, all of whom have the single goal of providing the best care, protection and
education to the children we help.
We help the needy children for the betterment of their tomorrow
